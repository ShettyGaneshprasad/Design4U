Frequently Asked Questions

What is Video KYC?

KYC or Know your customer is a key step in the Customer onboarding and authentication process of various Financial & insurance organizations. Video KYC is nothing but KYC via a video channel to streamline remote customer authentication at lower KYC costs.

What are the benefits of Video KYC Solution?

Video KYC Solution is a contactless KYC that brings down operational KYC costs for Financial & Insurance organizations and enables them to scale KYC processes with a tech driven infrastructure.

How is Ameyo’s Video based KYC Solution different?

Ameyo’s Video based KYC solution is more comprehensive than standalone Video KYC Solutions

  • Helps in reducing KYC Drop Offs at each stage of customer onboarding process with Omnichannel engagement 
  • Video KYC is capable of running on low internet bandwidth (60Kbps), thus providing a seamless onboarding experience to end customers
  • Provides Enterprise Grade Security and brings 15+ years of experience in dealing with Web Real Time communication channels
Is Ameyo Video KYC compliant ?

Ameyo’s Video KYC can be used by different organizations across banks, NBFC’s, Mutual funds , E-wallets, Broking, Insurance , Payments etc. We work with organizations to reach 100% compliance across RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, NHFL etc.

What Authentication process or document does Ameyo’s Video KYC Support?

Ameyo’s Video KYC Solution is flexible and supports PAN, AADHAAR, DL and any custom document required for compliance. Apart from Video based KYC authentication, Ameyo can also support e-KYC, XML based KYC processes based on the organization ask.
